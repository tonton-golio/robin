Goal: The tool library's Photo Boards writes shelf photos with a batch tag. On sync, the newest-batch photo moves to the front. No records change — a quick bridge, not a full donation-date overhaul.

Why: Volunteers keep pushing the newest donations' photos up by hand because the shelf order is by category, not by date. The batch tag is a quick bridge, not a records overhaul.
